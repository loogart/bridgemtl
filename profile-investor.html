What do we do for Quebec Entrepreneur Program (stream 1) candidates?

Investment opportunities

Research and presentation of investment opportunities as well as contractual assistance in the purchase process.

Research, analysis, presentations

Research, analysis and presentation to financial institutions for loans, and assistance for available grants and investments from institutional and private investors.

Immigration
process

Immigration process prepared and overlooked by an immigration lawyer, for the Quebec Entrepreneur Program, Stream 2.

Drafting a business plan

Drafting of a business plan to be used in the immigration application, taking into consideration your strengths and the immigration prerequisites.

Business
concierge

Personal concierge to help you settle in Montreal with your family. From finding the right schools, connecting with a trusted real estate agent, to choosing the best winter coats, we help you in a smooth landing.

Professional network access

Access to professional network: human resources, legal, financial, strategic and accounting counselling for the business

Coworking
space

Coworking space for business immigrants and local entrepreneurs to grow their businesses, in the heart of the Old Port of Montreal.

Networking

Networking with trusted local ecosystem, from suppliers to potential clients– our team’s collective network is yours.

Business coaching and councelling

Business coaching and counselling, guiding your business transition in Montreal. You will benefit from an experienced entrepreneur who will coach and follow-up to implement the business plan in the new environment. You may also, if required, assist in classes on doing business in Quebec.

Conditions

Conditions You must acquire a business for CAD 200,000, if outside of Montreal, or CAD 300,000, if in Montreal. The purchase has to be on a controlling (51%) share of the capital of a company existing for at least 5 years.

You must leave a security deposit of CAD 200,000 in a Canadian bank until your business plan is carried out.

You must prove a minimum net worth of CAD 900,000.

You must obtain, by the prescribed deadline, an attestation of learning about democratic values and the Québec values. The members of your family accompanying you (your spouse of 16 years of age or older and your dependent children of 18 years of age or older) must also obtain the attestation.

Full conditions: https://www.immigration- quebec.gouv.qc.ca/en/immigrate-settle/businesspeople/applying- business-immigrant/three-programs/entrepreneurs.html

QEP Stream 2 process

  • A. Signature of agreement and payment of USD 20,000
  • B. Choose a business and budget

    The first step we work on together is to choose a business sector and budget to focus on. At this point, we start connecting with our network to find a target. We will present you with options, of which you will choose one.

  • C. Conditional offer.

    Once a suitable business is found, a conditional offer can be drafted and presented.

  • D. Payment of USD 20,000, at acceptance of general terms of purchase.
  • E. Documentation gathering and application submission to Quebec Immigration Authorities (“MIFI”).

    You must collaborate with BridgeMTL immigration lawyers to provide an extended list of documents. At this point, we focus on the drafting of a viable business plan. In parallel, the immigration application is completed together. BridgeMTL’s immigration legal team will complete it and will need your collaboration to fill gaps in the information requested from the immigration authorities.

  • F. Selection interview and/or prospective trip in Quebec.

    You may be convened to assist a selection interview in Montreal assisted by the lawyer at BridgeMTL. You can also visit Montreal/Quebec to explore the local market, all supervised by BridgeMTL.

  • G. The immigration authorities will review your file and allocate a file number

    You may be convened to assist a selection interview in Montreal assisted by the lawyer at BridgeMTL. You can also visit Montreal/Quebec to explore the local market, all supervised by BridgeMTL.

  • H. Payment of USD 10,000 on allocation of a file number.
  • I. Decision

    Upon review, the immigration agent will either accept your file as is or ask for clarifications. In such case, the BridgeMTL legal will assist in answering questions to assure the file is accepted. The immigration agent may also refuse the file. In that case, if the BridgeMTL legal department believes that the MIFI has failed to meet its obligations, an administrative appeal will be submitted to overturn the outcome at no additional fees.

  • J. C.S.Q. issuance and permanent residence application

    C.S.Q. issuance and permanent residence application. As soon as your CSQ is issued, the BridgeMTL legal team will submit his/her PR application to federal authorities as well as his/her work permit if desired. The Quebec government, upon acceptance, will issue a CSQ (Certificat de selection du Québec), which means they accept your file, and your file is forwarded to the Canadian government (IRCC) for permanent residence and work permit if required.

  • K. Payment of USD 10,000 on issuance of the CSQ
  • L. Visa for visit.

    If a visa is necessary for a visit to Montreal, the BridgeMTL legal team will apply for the visitor visa for you, at no extra cost. If you wish to come to Montreal to work, they can also request a work visa, also at no extra fee.

  • M. Business project implementation.

    Business project implementation. You land in Montreal with either a temporary status (work permit) or permanent residence. BridgeMTL provides your office space and support, as well as personal assistance and guidance. When you arrive in Quebec, we will help you as needed to find a temporary or permanent home, schools for your children, suggestions and recommendations of all sorts. From a business perspective, you now have access to your office in the historic center of the city of Montreal, as well as help with financing, coaching, networking, business development, etc.

  • N. Business transition with our support.

    Implementing the business plan is an essential condition to get your security deposit back. We assist in making sure it goes as planned. For the next 12 months, our team will allocate all its resources to make sure your business plan is converted into reality. In parallel, BridgeMTL’s legal team will request your Permanent Residency with the Canadian immigration authorities.

  • O. Final payment of USD 10,000, at issuance of the Permanent Residency
  • P. BridgeMTL Alumni.

    You can decide to stay within BridgeMTL or start operating on its own.

Total cost: USD 70,000* not including government fees, official documents to translate and notarize, etc.
