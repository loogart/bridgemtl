About us

BridgeMTL is an immigration consultancy specialized in the Quebec Entrepreneur Program – assisting business immigrants in their immigration process. The BridgeMTL Incubator, a distinct non-profit organization, supports business immigrants in the successful launch of their project in Quebec.

The BridgeMTL consultancy will provide all services related to the immigration process, all delivered and overlooked by our legal team.

The incubator’s role is to demonstrate to the immigration authorities that it has the ability to support you with your project. To this end, BridgeMTL Incubator will draft a service offer, which is an essential part of the immigration file to be presented.

We put to our client’s disposal all of our team’s collective knowledge, resources and network to ensure that you will first successfully comply with government prerequisites, and then and most importantly, succeed in its business venture in Montreal.
